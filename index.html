Francesca Morfini

Hi, I am Francesca. I am a PhD Candidate in Psychology at Northeastern University in the EPIC Lab.



My research focuses on applying multimodal neuroimaging techniques (structural and funcitonal MRI) and machine learning to clinical and cognitive neuroscience. Specifically, my works aims to better characterize depression and anxiety in adolescence and youth, improve the diagnostic process, and develop novel treatment interventions.
